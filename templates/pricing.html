FAQ

Common Questions

Is this a subscription?
No. Every plan is a one-time purchase. You pay once and keep access for as long as the service is operated. There are no recurring charges, renewals, or hidden fees.
What do I get immediately after purchase?
Access is activated instantly after payment is confirmed. You will also receive a confirmation email with your order reference. Log in and open the engine — your new tier will be active.
Can I upgrade from Beginner to Pro later?
Yes. If you start on Beginner and later want Pro, you can purchase the Pro tier and your account will be upgraded. You only pay the difference in product access — upgrade any time.
What are Decision Credits?
Credits are a pay-as-you-go option. Each analysis you run costs one credit. Credits use Quick Mode with core engine features (win rate, EV, board texture). They never expire and can be topped up any time, with or without a plan.
Does EquiEdge AI guarantee I will win money?
No. EquiEdge AI is a decision-support tool that provides statistically optimal recommendations based on the inputs you provide. Poker involves variance — even correct decisions lose sometimes. The engine helps you make better decisions; results depend on many factors outside its control.
What if I need help or have a problem?
Contact us via the Contact page or email support@equiedgeai.io directly. We aim to respond within 24–48 hours.
Is payment secure?
Yes. Payments are processed by PayPal — we never see or store your card details. PayPal uses industry-standard encryption and fraud protection. Your financial information stays between you and PayPal.
Can I get a refund?
All purchases are final and non-refundable, as stated in our Terms of Service. If you experience a technical issue that prevents access, contact support and we will investigate and resolve it.
